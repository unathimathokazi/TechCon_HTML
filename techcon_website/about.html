History

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a gathering of top minds in technology. Over the years, it has grown to be one of the most anticipated conferences worldwide.

Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation, diversity, and collaboration in the global tech community by connecting thought leaders, innovators, and learners.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential figures in tech, including:

  • Ada Lovelace - Pioneering computer programmer who inspired our focus on innovation.
  • Alan Turing - His legacy in AI and computing is honored at every TechCon.
